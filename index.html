���� About This App

MyClass1TrainingApp is a self-guided CDL Class 1 (Commercial Driver's Licence) training simulator designed to help student drivers study, practise, and prepare for their written and practical exams — right from their phone or tablet.

Work through each module in order using Start Training Sequence, or jump to any module directly. No internet connection required once loaded.

🛑
Air Brakes — Interactive truck dashboard simulator. Practice the full CDL air brake pre-trip test: build pressure, governor cut-out, applied leak test (60 s timer), low-air warning (~60 PSI), and spring brake pop-out (~30 PSI). Includes a penalty scoring system in Test Mode.
🔧
Components Test — Study the complete air brake system component encyclopedia (compressor, governor, slack adjusters, chambers, ABS, glad hands, etc.) with search & category filter, then test yourself with a 10-question scored quiz and scenario trainer.
📋
Pre-Trip Inspection — Full interactive CDL pre-trip checklist across 8 vehicle zones (front, cab, fuel, drive tires, coupling/fifth wheel with tug test, trailer, trailer tires, rear). Tap each zone on the truck diagram and check off every item.
🚛
Drive — Open-world city driving simulator with a full road grid, traffic lights, crosswalks, street signs, a driver training yard, GPS navigation, night mode with headlights, and an examiner scoring system (lane keeping, red lights, speed).

💾 What Gets Saved (localStorage)

ixTemplate Your custom intersection layout from the Drive module's Intersection Editor (traffic light & sign positions/rotations).
startPose Your saved truck & trailer start position/angle in the Drive module (set by pressing SAVE START).

⚠️ All data is saved to your browser's localStorage on this device only. Clearing your browser data or site data will erase saved positions and intersection layouts. No account or internet connection is required.
